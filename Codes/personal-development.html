Applying Lessons from Twelve Pillars

Success is built on discipline, the right mindset, and continuous learning. In Twelve Pillars by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener, Chapters 4-7 emphasize key principles that align with my journey as a Computer Science student and aspiring Data Analyst.

One major lesson is discipline—small, consistent efforts lead to success. Mastering programming and data analysis requires daily practice, and applying discipline to my studies ensures steady progress. Another key principle is surrounding yourself with the right people. Engaging with experienced professionals and mentors will provide valuable insights and opportunities in my career.
